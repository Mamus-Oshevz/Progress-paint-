Our Paint Finishes

Emulsion

A classic water-based paint with a smooth, durable finish. It's ideal for interior walls and ceilings, offering excellent coverage and a washable surface.

Polychrome

Creates a unique, multi-colored textured effect. This paint is perfect for making a statement on feature walls, adding depth and character to any room.

Emulsion Matt

A non-reflective, flat finish that helps to hide minor imperfections on walls. It provides a modern, sophisticated look and a velvety texture.

Gloss/Oil

A highly durable, high-shine paint. It is resistant to scuffs, stains, and moisture, making it excellent for high-traffic areas like doors, trim, and kitchens.

Satin

Offers a soft, subtle sheen that is more durable and easier to clean than matt finishes. A popular choice for a wide range of interior surfaces.

Aluminium

A metallic paint that provides a sleek, silver finish. It is ideal for use on metal surfaces, offering both protection and a stylish, modern look.

White Gum

A thick, protective coating that offers excellent waterproofing and durability. It's perfect for sealing surfaces and protecting against harsh weather conditions.

Metallic Sparkles

Add a touch of glamour with our sparkling metallic paints. Available in various colors, they create a dazzling, eye-catching finish for any project.
